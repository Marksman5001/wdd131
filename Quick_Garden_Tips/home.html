Have you ever wondered if you could grow a plant indoors? Maybe you aren't sure if you've planted something in the right spot in your garden? This website is designed to provide information for you to be able to plant your fruits and vegitables better and help you to know what and where to plant!Whether you’re working with a sunny windowsill, a small balcony, or a spacious backyard, knowing the right conditions for each plant can make all the difference. Some fruits and vegetables thrive in containers indoors, while others need the full sun and space of an outdoor garden. Here, you’ll find tips on how much light, water, and care your plants need, along with advice on soil types, container sizes, and when to harvest. Whether you’re a beginner just starting out or an experienced gardener looking to try something new, this site will guide you step by step to help your garden grow strong, healthy, and delicious

Our information is on the Plant Information page. Select from one of the plants, select whether it will be planted indoors or outdoors, and press the Go! button to see the information provided!
